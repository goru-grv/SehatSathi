FAQ

1

How can I use the bot?

You can ask health-related questions using voice or text in your local language. The bot will instantly provide trusted, confidential guidance based on your symptoms, medicine availability, or government schemes.

2

Is my data safe?

Yes. SEHATRA uses secure systems to protect your personal information. Your health queries are processed privately and never shared without consent.

3

Can I access it without internet?

SEHATRA requires an internet connection to process queries and deliver accurate responses. However, offline features are being explored for future updates.

4

Which languages are supported?

You can interact in Hindi,English and More regional languages will be added soon to make SEHATRA accessible to every village.
