Symptom Checker

The symptom checker operates by using artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms to understand user-reported symptoms via natural language processing (NLP).8 Rather than a diagnostic tool, it serves as an "intelligent side-kick" to a medical professional, performing a "preliminary analysis of the case and present[ing] it to the doctor".4 This feature acts as the primary user acquisition and triage mechanism, providing immediate utility and guiding the user to the appropriate next step. The underlying technology is not limited to symptom analysis; it can be trained to analyze comprehensive datasets, including information from wearable devices, and identify critical health issues before a patient is even aware of symptoms.9 This predictive capability provides the technological foundation for the platform's emergency alert system, creating a direct and powerful link between the user's initial self-assessment and a potentially life-saving intervention.
